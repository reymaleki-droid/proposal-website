Package B

WordPress + Custom UI

A custom-designed WordPress site with unique visual identity, multi-level product categories, attribute filtering, and structured specification tables. Your brand, not a template.

Who This Is For

A business that wants a distinctive visual identity on WordPress. Has 100–500 products. Wants category-based browsing with filtering. Needs branded design but does not need CRM integration or automation. Has someone on staff who can manage WordPress content regularly.

What Is Included

  • Everything in Package A, plus:
  • Custom-designed WordPress theme (unique to client)
  • Design phase: wireframes, 2 rounds of revision, final mockups
  • Custom product category structure (multi-level, up to 3 levels deep)
  • Product filtering by category, brand, and up to 3 custom attributes
  • Product specification tables (structured per product type)
  • Product inquiry form per product (routes to designated email)
  • Custom header/footer with client branding
  • Up to 10 pages
  • Product entries: up to 200 products
  • Basic performance optimization (image compression, caching plugin)
  • 60-day post-launch bug fix window

What Is Excluded

  • CRM integration
  • Product import automation or bulk management tools
  • Telegram bot
  • E-commerce / payment processing
  • Multi-language support
  • Product comparison interface (side-by-side)
  • API endpoints for external systems
  • Advanced search (full-text, fuzzy matching)
  • Ongoing content updates beyond training
